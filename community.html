Mission

To create a centralized platform where STEM students across British Columbia can discover educational opportunities, competitions, scholarships, and programs at the right time, addressing the challenge of finding timely STEM resources.

Origin Story

BC STEM Opportunities emerged from personal experience and community need. As STEM students ourselves, we recognized how challenging it was to find relevant opportunities at the right time. Many students miss out on competitions, programs, and scholarships simply because they don't know about them or find out too late.

While working on Impactivate Engineering, we realized that creating a centralized resource hub could solve this fundamental problem for students across British Columbia. This platform represents our commitment to ensuring no student misses out on STEM opportunities due to lack of information or poor timing.

Future Vision

Expanding Impact

Our work in community impact is just beginning. Through Impactivate Engineering and BC STEM Opportunities, we're building the foundation for a more equitable and accessible STEM education ecosystem in British Columbia.

Future plans include expanding our teaching programs, developing innovative project-based learning initiatives, and creating province-wide partnerships that will reach even more students in underserved communities.

"Every student deserves access to quality STEM education and opportunities. Through community-driven initiatives, we can break down barriers and inspire the next generation of innovators."
