Early Voices of Parkinson's

Project Type Original Research
Achievement CWSF Qualification (Top 2%)
Recognition Ingenious+ $1000 Award
Performance 98.3% Accuracy, 0 False Positives

Abstract

AI system that analyzes voice patterns to detect Parkinson's disease long before traditional diagnostic methods, with 98.3% accuracy and zero false positives.

Approach

Developed a hybrid AI system that combines:

  • Traditional ML with voice feature extraction
  • Deep learning trained on voice spectrograms
  • A fusion layer integrating both methods

Key Findings

  • Voice biomarkers detectable before visible symptoms appear
  • 98.3% accuracy across languages and accents
  • Strong correlation between voice patterns and disease progression

Applications

  • Early detection leading to better clinical outcomes
  • Accessible smartphone-based screening for underserved communities
  • Non-invasive monitoring of disease progression
  • Framework for detecting other neurological conditions
